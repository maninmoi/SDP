Tutorial

Sign up:

You need to create an account to use the website. Only username, email, and password are required.


Sign in:

Enter your email and password to sign in.


Select a dataset:

Click on it to open a dropdown menu to choose datasets.


Options:

Different datasets you can choose to visualise.


Visualisation:

The histogram visualises the jobs-in-data dataset in this example. The arrow shows a data point of a friend.


Parameters:

Datasets offer parametrs to input your own data. There are 2 different types of parameters: Textboxes and Selectfields.


Friendlist:

Shows all friends of the user.


Friends pending:

Shows all pending incoming friend requests. The check allows to accept the request and the cross allows to deny it.


Friend request:

You can send other users friend requests. Just input there username and click send.
